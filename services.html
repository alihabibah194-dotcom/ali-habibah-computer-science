Services for Students

Below are the main services I offer to help students get more involved in politics and civic life.

Service List

  • One-on-one mentorship and advising.
  • Small group workshops and training sessions.
  • Resume and cover letter review for political internships.
  • Help finding volunteer and campaign opportunities.

Service Details

Service Description Best For
Mentorship Discuss your goals, interests, and create a custom plan to get involved. Students just starting to explore politics.
Workshops Interactive sessions on campaigning, voting, and public speaking. Students who want practical skills.
Campaign Training Learn how to canvass, phone bank, and support real campaigns. Students ready for hands-on experience.
