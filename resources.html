Resources for Getting Into Politics

Here are some helpful websites and ideas to help you take your next step into politics.

Educational Resources

  • USA.gov - Voting and Elections
  • Congress.gov - Learn about bills and laws.
  • Ballotpedia - Elections and candidate information.

Ways to Get Involved

  1. Join a student government or political club on campus.
  2. Volunteer for a local campaign or community organization.
  3. Attend town halls, debates, and public meetings.
  4. Register to vote and help friends register too.
