Welcome to Ali's Political Pathways

Hello! My name is Ali, and I am a Political Science major dedicated to helping students get involved in politics, understand how government works, and find their voice in public life.

What Is Ali's Political Pathways?

Ali's Political Pathways is a student-focused initiative that helps young people:

  • Learn the basics of political systems and public policy.
  • Connect with local political organizations and campus groups.
  • Find internships, fellowships, and volunteer opportunities.
  • Develop leadership, communication, and advocacy skills.

Our Core Programs

  1. Mentorship: One-on-one guidance to help students build a path into politics.
  2. Workshops: Practical sessions on campaigning, public speaking, and civic engagement.
  3. Political Campaign Training: Hands-on training on canvassing, phone banking, and field work.

Program Overview

Program Level Focus
Mentorship Beginner Finding your interests in politics, goal-setting, and networking.
Workshops Intermediate Skills like public speaking, debating, and understanding policy issues.
Campaign Training Advanced Practical experience with campaigns, organizing events, and outreach.
