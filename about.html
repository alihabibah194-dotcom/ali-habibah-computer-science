About Ali

My name is Ali, and I am a Political Science major who believes that students should have a clear path into politics. Growing up, I saw how confusing elections, laws, and government could be, especially for young people and first-time voters.

I started Ali's Political Pathways to make politics less intimidating and more accessible. My goal is to help students understand how decisions are made, how to participate, and how to become leaders in their communities.
